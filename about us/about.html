Our Focus Areas

We work across essential sectors to create sustainable impact, empower communities, and improve quality of life.

Education

Education is the foundation of a brighter future. We support children and youth through quality learning, school assistance, and skill development to break the cycle of poverty.

Health

Good health is a basic human right. We improve community well-being through medical camps, preventive care awareness, nutrition support, and accessible healthcare services.

Waste Management

We promote responsible waste management through segregation, recycling initiatives, and environmental awareness to build cleaner, healthier, and sustainable communities.

Who We Are

USTHI FOUNDATION INDIA (Cares for Blossoms in the Dust)

USTHI Foundation India established in the year 1977 is a non-profit social welfare program devoted to improving humanitarian aid and support for men, women and children belonging to underprivileged sections of society.

The organization started its work in a remote village of South 24 Parganas with a mission to create a sustainable and equitable society where everyone has an opportunity to thrive with meaningful contribution.

Today it operates programs on education, community health, vocational training and disaster relief across Odisha and West Bengal.
